events

This is the events script custom written for ####. Its purpose is to allow people to share events with the community without pesky registration / tracking / paywalls.

Try checking out the events or making your own. There is an option to make people enter a password to view your event; to share private events with people, enter the password, and copy the URL of the "unlocked" event.

Rules and guidelines:

  1. Don't spam
  2. Include a relevant event description and contact information, if necessary
  3. Contact me for moderation concerns
